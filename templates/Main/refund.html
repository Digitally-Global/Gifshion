Refund Policy

Refund Process: To initiate a refund, please follow these steps: a. Contact our customer support team or visit our website to request a refund. b. Provide the necessary details such as your order number, product information, and reason for the refund. c. Our customer support team will guide you through the necessary steps and provide you with a refund authorization. d. Conditions for Refunds: To be eligible for a refund, please ensure that the following conditions are met: a. The refund request should be initiated within the specified time frame, typically within 15 days from the date of delivery. In some cases, we may require the returned item to be shipped back to us. Please contact our customer support team for specific instructions regarding return shipments. Refunds for Canceled Orders: If your order is canceled for any reason, such as unavailability of the product or failure to process the payment, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method. You will be notified of the cancellation and refund process. Refunds for Faulty or Damaged Products: If you receive a faulty or damaged product, please contact our customer support team immediately with relevant details and supporting evidence (such as photographs). We will assess the situation and, if necessary, arrange for a return and provide you with a refund or replacement. Please note that these refund policies are subject to change. We encourage you to review our refund policy periodically for any updates or changes. If you have any questions or require assistance regarding our refund policy, please reach out to our customer support team.
